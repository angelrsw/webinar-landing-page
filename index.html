Frequently asked

Quick answers before you sign up

Are the webinars really free?
Yes — 100% free, with no limit on the number of attendees from your store. You can join as many sessions as you like. We ask that you register at least 24 hours in advance so we can send you the Zoom link and confirm your spot.
Are webinars recorded?
No — our live webinars aren't recorded. Each session is shaped by the questions and interactions of the attendees, which makes every one different. If you want recorded deep-dive content, check out our Liberty Training Videos on YouTube, or book a Private Remote Training session so we can record it for your team.
What do I need to attend a webinar?
A computer with a working internet connection and Zoom installed (desktop app recommended). After you register, you'll receive an email with the join link and all the details. That's it — no software downloads, no setup on our end.
What's covered in the 3-Day Masterclass?
The Masterclass walks you through the full life of an item — from the moment it enters your store to the final consignor payout. You'll cover setup, POS, pricing, inventory, reports, payouts, and online listings. It's available both virtually and in-person at our Orlando HQ.
How does Private Remote Training work?
You tell us what you want to learn — messy reports, Shopify setup, year-end reconciliation — and we book a 1:1 Zoom session with a Liberty expert. Training is billed hourly at $100/hour, and you only pay for the time you book. Great for teams who want focused, on-demand help.
What's included in On-Site Training?
A Resaleworld expert flies to your store for a full day of in-person training. We work with your entire team, fix specific workflows on the spot, set up equipment, and leave you with documented best practices. Billed at $1,000/day plus travel and per diem. Contact us for a quote.
Can I mix and match training options?
Absolutely — and we recommend it. Most stores start with free webinars, send a manager to the Masterclass, book private sessions when specific issues come up, and schedule on-site training once a year for a deep team reset. All four tiers work together.
